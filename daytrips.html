Historical Trips

Drombeg Stone Circle

Drombeg Stone Circle, an ancient marvel in County Cork, Ireland, dates back to the Bronze Age. Nestled in a serene landscape, this mystical site comprises 17 standing stones forming a ceremonial circle. Known as the "Druid's Altar," it's aligned with the winter solstice sunset. Visitors marvel at its archaeological significance, pondering its purpose in rituals or celestial observations. Surrounded by folklore and mystery, it remains a compelling testament to Ireland's enigmatic past and spiritual heritage..

Bantry House

Bantry House, a majestic estate nestled on Ireland's southwest coast, exudes timeless grandeur. Steeped in history since the late 17th century, its elegant architecture, resplendent gardens, and captivating views of Bantry Bay enchant visitors. The house, adorned with exquisite art, antique treasures, and period furniture, offers a vivid glimpse into the past. Preserved by the Shelswell-White family, its allure lies not just in its beauty but also in the welcoming embrace that invites all to explore its rich heritage.

Mountain Walks and Hikes

Hungry Hill

Hungry Hill, a majestic peak in County Cork's Caha Mountains, captivates with its rugged beauty and towering presence. The second-highest mountain in this range, its summit offers panoramic views of Bantry Bay and the surrounding countryside. Steeped in folklore from Daphne du Maurier's novel, it holds a mythical allure. Adventurous hikers and nature enthusiasts flock to its trails, seeking breathtaking vistas and a glimpse into the mystical tales that shroud this iconic Irish landmark.

Sheeps Head Lighthouse

The Sheep's Head Walking Loop, a scenic trail along Ireland's Wild Atlantic Way, meanders across the Sheep's Head Peninsula. Enveloped in natural beauty, it unveils lush landscapes, coastal cliffs, and quaint villages. The loop's highlight, the iconic Sheep's Head Lighthouse, stands proudly on craggy cliffs, offering mesmerizing sea views. Hikers revel in the serene ambience, relishing the coastal panoramas and the rugged charm of this trail, an idyllic haven for nature lovers and adventurers alike.
